Post-Construction

Our Project & Construction Management Services do not end when construction is complete. We continue working with our clients to guarantee that things go smoothly even after the site is occupied.— we strive to build & maintain relationships & ensure complete satisfaction. Receive & coordinate (with the Contractor) all close-out items including:as-built drawings, operations & maintenance manuals, & warranties as required. Prepare a final close-out report with recommendations . regarding final payment, notice of completion, & file system for retrieval of close-out documentation. Assist with commissioning of new building & providing necessary on-site training for the building’s operational system.
