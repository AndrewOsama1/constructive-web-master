Constructive is responsible for

  • The daily follow up & micro management for all the running activities of 12 villas with contractors.
  • The assurance of applying the Health and Safety standards in all the running projects to maintain a safe working environment and a safe living environment for the Residents.
  • Establishing a well-studied reporting system with different layers of information serving as a top and a mid-management reports. Starting with an overview of the projects’ progress status and rates and ending micromanagement processes and corrective actions taken by the Contractors.
  • Co-ordination between several Contractors by Creating and applying a well-studied water tight time schedules to achieve the ultimate results.
  • Extracting permits for the excavation and building of slightly less than 300 units.
  • Design modification and review for main entrances crossing for the compound, and their intersection with infrastructure pipelines.

    • The location of the project is very distinctive because it is on an important central road, which is the Oasis Road and the Nile Sat Road behind the Media Production City. Which built trust with the owner to award constructive the permits for excavation and building of 283 residential units along with the design modification of main entrances crossing for the compound, and their intersection with infrastructure pipelines.
