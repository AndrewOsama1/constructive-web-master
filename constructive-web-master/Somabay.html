Project

SomaBay

Somabay is a coastal resort on the Red Sea 45 km from Hurghada International airport.
The 10 M square meters self contained community of Somabay is a peninsula surrounded by the sea all around.

Constructive is the Consultancy and Project management company overseeing all projects within Soma Bay boundaries. We are currently supervising and managing over 15 zones with around 40 projects in their different phases starting from Tendering phase, Construction phase till handing-over and Customers satisfaction.

Constructive consists of 2 main departments, Project Controls Team and Supervision Team. Both Teams are integrated and connected to achieve full control over all project in all aspects, starting from Contractors short listing, passing through technical and financial evaluation, Quality Control and Assurance, following up Works on site till the delivery of the Projects on time and with the highest quality.
