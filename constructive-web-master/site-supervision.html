Site Supervision

Our responsibilities will include but will not be limited to the following: The supervision of all construction work, as we will administer the Construction Contract and ensure that the contractual clauses, with respect to both quality and quantity of work are respected and the works are constructed in accordance with the provisions of the Construction Contracts. The nomination of a Representative who will be full time resident on the Project. The reviewal of the Construction Contracts for the purpose of identifying any omissions, which compromise the completeness or consistency of the design.

  1. To certify completion of part or all the works.
  2. To ensure that the project is executed according to the designs, drawings and specifications specified in the bidding documents / applicable standards, and that good engineering practices are followed in construction.
  3. To ensure that the project is executed following the relevant laws / statutes and practices / guidelines related to workers welfare, safety at worksite, insurances, etc.
  4. To inspect, review and report the adequacy and competence of contractor’s staff, labor and machinery. The reviewing of the contractor’s work program and advice on need for corrective measures in cases where such matters are referred by the Engineers

Site Supervision and Quality control department depend on the experience exerted to reach optimum results. which is matching with the international codes and standards.

Site supervision includes:
  • Detailed review of the contractors’ quality plan and health & safety plan.
  • Survey works
  • Evaluate and approve contractors’ technical submitting inclusive of engineering drawings, materials and method statements.
  • Following up on all activities performed by the contractor on site.
